Mike Trout

Mike Trout is a 30 year old, single man, and is the CEO of Harvest Energy Solutions, a company that he built where he and his employees research ways in which energy can be harvested at the most efficient levels.

Facts

Mike currently oversees the research and results of dozens of employees who are either out in the field or working in the lab. He currently lives in Detroit, MI, where Harvest Energy Solutions headquarters are located.

Interests and Values

Mike spends most of his time at the office reviewing work, but when he's not at the office, he's spending time with the five year old Golden Retriever, Rosco. Mike also loves going around the state and giving lectures on harvesting energy.

Computer, Internet, and TV Use

Mike currently owns two Macbook Air laptops, and two brand new iPhone X's. One of each at home, and one of each at work. He also owns a Kindle, and an iPad. He's typically use these for work purposes such as research, review, and business strategies, and when he's not using it for these, he's reading articles for fun on new advances in interstellar technology.

A Typical Day

  • Mike gets up every morning at 6 am to take Rosco out for a walk. He then eats breakfast and heads to work around 7:30am.
  • When he gets to work, he runs a meeting with all of his employees to make sure everyone knows where to go what to do for the day.
  • He then typically has meetings from 10 am until around 2 or 3 pm. When he gets back, he goes around the lab to make sure everything is going as planned.
  • Around 4 pm every day, he calls his employees out in the field to check in on their progress.
  • He usually gets finsished with his phone calls around 6 pm, and then he goes home to do his own research on other companies and individuals who do similar work, hoping he finds someone who he can share his knowledge with.
  • He then eats a late dinner, and heads to bed around 11 pm

Future Goals

Mike hopes to find other companies and individuals in the energy harvesting field where he can work with and combine ideas that will benefit both of their specific companies/ideas. He would even be willing to merge his company with another if he is really intrigued by their work and where they are headed.

Quote

"Harvesting energy is extremeley important. I'm hoping that in the near future, we are able to figure out solutions to where we can efficiently harvest enough energy to travel from solar system to solar system"
